What is StockFlow?


A Web application that allows you to create multiple wallets and organize your stocks in your own way. The stocks values of the most diverse companies are presented in a clear and detailed way. You can view a chart with the latest stocks values, latest company news and also if it is a good time to buy or sell your stock.
